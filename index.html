Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I place an order?

We recommend placing your order at least 3-4 days in advance for customized cakes, and 1 week prior for large tier or wedding cakes.

Do you deliver across Delhi NCR?

Yes! We are based in Gurgaon but we safely deliver our cakes across Delhi, Gurgaon, and Noida using premium AC cab delivery services.

Do you make 100% eggless cakes?

Absolutely. We specialize in both egg and 100% eggless bakes, completely customized to your dietary preferences.

Can I customize the flavor and design?

Yes, all our celebration cakes are fully customized. Share your theme, colors, and references in the contact form, and we will bring it to life!
